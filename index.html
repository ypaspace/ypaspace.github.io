Welcome

Hi there! A Guide to Airliners for Kids is a website where you can learn almost anything about common airliners made by Boeing and Airbus for kids like you! This website includes a detailed page on each aircraft and manufactuer (work in progress!). To start reading press Introduction on the left hand side! Click on news to find out about some recent aviation news and List of Airliners to look at all the common airliners made by Boeing and Airbus.
