Boeing 717

Devolpement

The Boeing 717 was not oringinally a Boeing plane, it was made by McDonald Douglas.When the 717 was produced by McDonald Douglas it was known as the MD-95. The 717 only became a Boeing plane when Boeing took over McDonald Douglas in 1997. From 1995, when the first 717 was delivered by McDonald Douglas, to 2006, when the last 717 was produced, all 155 ordered were delivered.
